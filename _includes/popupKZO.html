I am not associated with Keizaal Online.

Hello, I'm Tate Taylor, you may know me from mods such as C.O.I.N., Gladys the Corgi, and Skyrim: Extended Cut - Saints and Seducers. You may also know me from my Wabbajack modlist Keizaal, which is the oldest modlist still available for download on the platform. As I approach the full release of Keizaal 9, I wanted to clear a few things up.

Over the past six months or so the Skyrim mutiplayer project known as Keizaal Online has exploded in popularity. Due to us sharing names, I've come across a ton of people (including some friends!) assuming that I am somehow involved with this project. I just want to make it clear that I am not associated with Keizaal Online.

To be honest, Keizaal Online has been causing quite a few headaches for me. Not only has it completely destroyed the ability to easily search for my modlist online but now a stream of people have been bothering me about problems associated with Keizaal Online.

About a month ago someone DM'd me begging for me to unban them despite how many times I told them I had nothing to do with Keizaal Online. This is just one of many instances that have come out of this confusion. I've since built a filter to prevent lost Keizaal Online users from entering my Discord server. I don't like doing this, but the alternative was to continue letting these people waste my time.

I really wish they hadn't named their project the same as my mine. I've been working on Keizaal for six years and this is one of the most annoying things to come from my time curating it.

Anyway, I just wanted to clear that up. I'm sure this won't stop the confusion, but I at least wanted to get some eyes on it to inform more members of the community.

Thank you for reading!
